Worm-Burner Lacrosse

Worm-Burner is committed to providing affordable access to lacrosse equipment, teams, and leagues.


We believe that lacrosse should be accessible to anyone, so we offer:

Affordable sticks, heads, and complete sets.

Affordable gear, including pads, helmets, cleats, etc.

Access to professional coaching and league services.
